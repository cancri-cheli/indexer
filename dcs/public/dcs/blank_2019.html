Akathist Canon: New Translation

With God's help, Fr. Seraphim Dedes has produced a fresh translation of the Akathist Canon that meets the poetic meter, the meaning, and the intent of the original Greek poem, while also emphasizing the various scriptural references and the imagery applied to the All-Holy Mother of God.

The new text and scores supersede the 2000 edition, which was a text produced over twenty years ago, and which received much criticism, especially in recent years. The new materials are now available, free of charge, on the Digital Chant Stand, on the Friday evenings of Lent.

An Orthodox Arts Journal article provides a detailed look at the approach of the new translation.
